San Francisco

San Francisco is the cultural, commercial, and financial center of Northern California and the only consolidated city-county in California.San Francisco is the fourth-most populous city in California, after Los Angeles, San Diego and San Jose.

San Francisco was founded on June 29, 1776. San Francisco is a popular tourist destination, known for its cool summers, fog, steep rolling hills, eclectic mix of architecture, and landmarks, including the Golden Gate Bridge.
